This is your semi-official guide.

This app is meant to be a quick introduction to MAPD program at Centennial College.

It was created by students as one of the assignments for the course. It was built with Ionic framework used for developing cross-platform apps and uses Google's Firebase as a backend

Do you want to build apps?

Browse through this app, look at module descriptions and professor profiles and check out some of the feedback left by program graduates.

Want to get details and apply?

For your capstone project you will work in teams to build a full-featured app for iOS or Android while learning Agile development methodology.
